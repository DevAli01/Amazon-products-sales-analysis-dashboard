Amazon Products Sales Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

📌 Project Introduction

This Power BI dashboard analyses Amazon product sales performance, providing insights into sales trends, product category performance, customer reviews, and product demand over time.

The dashboard focuses on YTD and QTD sales metrics, helping identify high-performing products, category contributions, and seasonal sales patterns.

🔎 Key Business Questions

What are the Year-to-Date (YTD) and Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales trends?

Which product categories generate the most revenue?

Which products drive the highest sales and customer reviews?

How do monthly and weekly sales patterns change throughout the year?

Which products contribute the most to overall performance?

🛠 Tools Used

Power BI Desktop

Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

DAX (Time Intelligence Calculations)

Data Modeling

Interactive Dashboard Design

📈 Key Insights

Total YTD Sales reached $2.18M, with $811K in QTD sales.

Men Shoes dominate sales contribution with over 43% of total revenue.

Sales show strong growth in the last quarter of the year.

Certain products generate extremely high review volumes, indicating strong customer engagement.

Weekly sales trends reveal spikes during peak shopping periods.
